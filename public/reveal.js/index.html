Groupseminar 19.04.17 ## Sources of anomalous diffusion for diffusion processes in hierarchical free energy landscape Andreas Volkhardt

Example: continuous time random walk (CTRW)

  • random walk process with dwell times in visited states
  • Variance is proportional to number of random walk steps $\sigma^2 \propto N$

Long tailed waiting time distributions cause subdiffusion

  • Central theorem applies: $N \propto T$ -> normal diffusion
  • When central limit theorem applies, also $N \propto T$ -> normal diffusion

Waiting times are not responsible for subdiffusion in 1-D

  • Simulation: $1000$ random walks, $N=10^4$ steps, $\Delta F = 3 [kT]$
  • Number of visits $N$ is proportional to the passed time $T$
